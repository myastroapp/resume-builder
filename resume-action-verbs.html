Replace these weak openers: "Responsible for…", "Worked on…", "Helped with…", "Duties included…", "Assisted in…". They describe presence, not impact. Swap in a verb above and add the outcome.

How to use them

  • One verb per bullet, in the past tense (present tense for your current role).
  • Don't repeat the same verb — vary across bullets.
  • Always pair the verb with a measurable result: a %, a $, a count, or a timeframe.
