What should a resignation letter include?

Keep it short and gracious: a clear statement that you're resigning, your last working day (usually two weeks out, or per your contract), a brief thank-you, and an offer to help with the handover. Don't air grievances — this letter goes in your file and you may need a reference.

How much notice should I give?

Two weeks is the common standard in the US; check your contract, as some roles require more. Give notice to your manager directly first, then send the written letter.
