Privacy Policy — Resume Builder

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This resume builder runs entirely in your browser. Everything you type — your contact details, experience, and education — stays on your device and is used only to render and print your resume.

What we collect

Nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your resume data is never sent to or stored on any server.

Payments

If you unlock Pro, payment is handled by Stripe; we never see or store your card details. The unlock is stored locally in your browser.

Changes

Any updates to this policy will be posted here.
